Crystals Collector

You will be given a target number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. When you click one you will add a specific number of points to your score.


When your score matches the target number, you win! If your score exceeds the random number, you lose.


The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. Use math!


Each time the game starts, the crystals will receive new values.


Good luck!
